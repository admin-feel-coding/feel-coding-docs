### Typical Clients

### Relationship Type

* High trust, high risk

### Payment Model

### How D2E Applies

* **O**: Usually postponed

### Rules (Very Important)

* Equity or free work is never automatic
* Clear exit conditions must exist

### Selection Criteria

Feel Coding considers this model only if:

* Strategic alignment exists
* Trust exists
* Long-term value outweighs short-term cost
* Risk is consciously accepted

### Doctrine

> Partnership is a **choice**, not a favor.
